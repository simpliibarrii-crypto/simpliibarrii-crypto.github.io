RESEARCH METHOD

01

Read primary sources

Start from papers, official repositories, specifications, and benchmark contracts rather than second-hand summaries.

02

Separate fact from inference

Mark what the source demonstrates, what the engineering translation assumes, and what remains unverified.

03

Build the smallest testable contract

Turn a research property into code, tests, data structures, or a deterministic browser demonstration.

04

Scope public claims

Label prototypes, drafts, integrations, and safety limitations plainly. Credibility is part of the engineering.
