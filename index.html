ENGINEERING APPROACH

Useful intelligence needs structure.

The work is organized around constraints that matter after the prototype glow fades.

01

Evidence before confidence

Claims should carry sources, risk labels, verification steps, and an inspectable trace.

02

Local before remote

Deterministic tools and on-device models handle suitable work before expensive cloud escalation.

03

Boundaries before autonomy

Consent, tenant isolation, policy gates, and human approval define what an agent is allowed to do.

04

Benchmarks before claims

Performance language is tied to device, backend, memory, thermal state, and reproducible metrics.
